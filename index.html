CS PhD student from 2016

Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST)


Biography: Renzhi Xiao is a PhD student majoring in computer system architecture in Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) advised by Prof.Dan Feng and Prof.Yuchong Hu. He received the B.E. degree in software engineering (Java Web) from Jiangxi University of Science and Technology, Nanchang, China, in 2013.

His research interests include computer architecture, in-memory key-value store, non-volatile memory, and PM-based data structures（Simulation or DCPMM）.
