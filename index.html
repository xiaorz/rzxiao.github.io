Biography: Renzhi Xiao is a Ph.D. student majoring in computer system architecture in Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) advised by Prof. Dan Feng. He has been a student member of the China Computer Federation (CCF) since September 2016. Formerly served as the monitor of Class 1604, Ph.D., Wuhan Optoelectronics National Laboratory, and a propagandist at CCF Huazhong University of Science and Technology. He received a B.E. degree in software engineering from Jiangxi University of Science and Technology (JXUST), Nanchang, China 2013.
