Renzhi Xiao

CS PhD student
Huazhong University of Science and Technology
E-mail: rzxiao@hust.edu.cn


Biography: Renzhi Xiao is a PhD student majoring in computer system architecture in Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) advised by Prof.Dan Feng and Prof.Yuchong Hu. He received the B.E. degree in software engineering from Jiangxi University of Science and Technology, Nanchang, China, in 2013.

His research interests include computer architecture, in-memory key-value store, non-volatile memory, and NVM-based data structures.
